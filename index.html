Bearing Witness is an effort to document and interpret the events associated with the novel coronavirus epidemic in the United States as it pertains to racialized minorities.

Our methodology is fairly simple. Our research team has established a series of Google alerts on specific topics related to mass incarceration, social determinants of health. The timeline represents principally four types of events: data events, political events, policy events, and scientific events. The Timeline is generated using TimelineJS, an application created by the Northwestern University Knight Lab. We are also providing a full bibliography to accompany the Timeline.
